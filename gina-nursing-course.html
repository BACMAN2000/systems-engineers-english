SBAR Technique — the standard way to communicate:

  • Situation: "Dr. Smith, this is Gina, the cardiology nurse on unit 3."
  • Background: "Mr. Johnson, 65, post-op day 2 after CABG."
  • Assessment: "His blood pressure dropped to 90/60, heart rate is 110."
  • Recommendation: "I think he may need a fluid bolus. Could you come assess him?"
